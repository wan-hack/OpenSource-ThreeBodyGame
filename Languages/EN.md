# Open Source Three-Body Game - First-Person Experience

## üåü Project Introduction
This is a **non-commercial, permanently free open-source game** initiated by a 6th-grade Three-Body fan, built together with global Three-Body fans.
We 1:1 recreate the Three-Body Game that Wang Miao played in the original novel, with a first-person perspective to experience the survival challenges of the Stable Era/Chaotic Era, restoring classic mechanics such as Dehydration/Rehydration, Three Suns in the Sky, and Flying Star warnings.

## üéÆ Core First-Person Gameplay
1.  **View**: First-person (you are the player in the game), in a simple village on the Three-Body Planet.
2.  **Era Rule**: Randomly start in Stable Era/Chaotic Era. A 5s red text prompt ("Chaotic Era is coming! Extreme heat, dehydrate quickly!") will appear before Chaotic Era.
3.  **Dehydration & Rehydration**:
    - Stable Era: Click yourself to rehydrate (resume activity) and collect water/ore resources.
    - Chaotic Era: Click yourself to dehydrate (become dry fiber, immune to heat), and rehydrate again after Stable Era resumes.
4.  **Win Condition**: Collect 3 mixed resources, go to the village observatory to unlock era prediction, and achieve "civilization survival".
